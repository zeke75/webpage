Alaska, July 2013

I'm a Ph.D. candidate in Mathematics with a specialization in Statistics at Department of Mathematics, University of California, San Diego. I'm scheduled to graduate in September 2017.

My research interests include:

  • Multivariate and functional time series
  • Resampling and bootstrap for dependent data
  • Nonparametric spectral density estimation
  • Time series anomaly detection
My advisor is Dimitris Politis.

My hometown is Shanghai, China and now I live in La Jolla, California. Before coming to UCSD, I received M.S. in Mathematics from Texas A&M University in 2012 and B.S. in Mathematics from Fudan University of China in 2010.
